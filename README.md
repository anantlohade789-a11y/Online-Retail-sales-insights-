# Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end sales analysis of an Online Retail dataset using **Microsoft Excel**. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive business dashboard that helps identify sales trends, customer behavior, geographical performance, and product-level insights.

The project demonstrates a complete Excel-based analytics workflow, including data cleaning, transformation, pivot table analysis, dashboard creation, and business insight reporting.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objectives of this project were to:

- Clean and prepare retail transaction data for analysis.
- Analyze sales performance across countries, customers, and invoices.
- Identify high-performing products and seasonal sales trends.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business users.
- Communicate findings through concise business insights.

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional online retail sales data, including information such as:

- Invoice Number
- Product Description
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Customer ID
- Invoice Date
- Country

The analysis focuses on understanding revenue generation, customer purchasing behavior, invoice patterns, product performance, and geographical sales distribution.

# Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

- The business generated approximately **$10.6 million** in total revenue.
- A total of **19,960 invoices** were processed.
- The average invoice value was approximately **$533.17**.

### Sales Geography

- Sales were recorded across **38 countries**.
- Approximately **90%** of sales came from the domestic market.
- International markets contributed approximately **10%** of total revenue.

### Seasonality

- Sales activity was strongest between **September and November**, indicating increased customer demand during this period.
- **December 2011 appears to contain incomplete data**, so the observed decline at the end of the timeline should not be interpreted as evidence of reduced business performance without additional context.

### Product Performance

The highest revenue-generating products include:

- DOTCOM POSTAGE
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- PAPER CRAFT LITTLE BIRDIE

Together, these three products contribute approximately **47% of total sales**, highlighting a significant concentration of revenue among a small group of products.

# Business Communication

Based on the analysis:

- Continue monitoring high-performing products to maintain revenue contribution.
- Prepare inventory and operational capacity ahead of the September–November peak sales period.
- Explore opportunities to increase international sales, which currently represent approximately **10%** of total revenue.
- Regularly monitor customer purchasing patterns and invoice values to support pricing and sales strategies.
- Consider expanding successful product categories while evaluating dependence on a small number of top-selling products.
