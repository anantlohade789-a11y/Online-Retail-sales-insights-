# Online-Retail-sales-insights-
Excel bases retail sales analysis project with cleaned data, pivot tables, dashboard, insights and business record .
## 📌 Overall Conclusion

The business demonstrates **strong overall performance**, generating **$10.6M in total revenue** from **19,960 invoices** across **38 countries**, indicating a broad global customer base.

### Key Insights
- 💰 Total Revenue: **$10.6M**
- 🧾 Total Invoices: **19,960**
- 🌍 Operations across **38 countries**
- 📈 Sales peak during **September–November**, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- 🇬🇧 Revenue is heavily dependent on the **United Kingdom**, creating a **market concentration risk**.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- 🌎 **Expand international markets** to reduce dependence on the UK and diversify revenue streams.
- 📦 **Diversify the product catalog** by introducing new products and strengthening underperforming categories.
- 📊 **Improve inventory planning** by forecasting demand based on seasonal sales trends, especially for the September–November peak period.
- 🎯 Focus marketing and sales efforts on high-growth international regions to increase customer acquisition and revenue diversification.

## 🚀 Final Takeaway

Overall, the company is financially healthy and shows strong seasonal sales performance. Future growth should focus on **geographic expansion**, **product diversification**, and **data-driven inventory planning** to reduce risk and support sustainable long-term growth.
